I'm currently a senior at Harvard University studying Applied Math - Computer Science with a minor in Statistics. I work in the AI4LIFE Professor Hima Lakkaraju's lab, and previously spent 1.5 years at IBM Research AI.

My work has been published at venues such as NeurIPS and has been awarded the ACM Cutler Bell Prize for Excellence in Computing, among others.

I'm broadly interested in interpretable and explainable AI techniques and Natural Language Processing, Reasoning, and Causal Inference.

I currently serve as Co-President of Harvard Women in CS. I've also served as a Teaching Fellow for Harvard's Applied Mathematics 120 – Linear Algebra for Big Data since 2020, efforts for which I've won Harvard's Derek Bok Prize for Excellence in Teaching.

My email is ishapuri [at] college [dot] harvard [dot] edu. I'd love to hear from you!
